Embedded Systems Engineer

We're seeking an embedded systems engineer to develop firmware for our next-generation IoT devices.

Requirements:
- Strong C/C++ programming skills
- Experience with microcontrollers and embedded Linux
- Understanding of I2C, SPI, UART, and other communication protocols
- Experience with sensor integration and data processing
- Knowledge of real-time operating systems
- Ability to read schematics and work closely with hardware team

Nice to have:
- Experience with ARM Cortex-M or similar MCUs
- 3D visualization and data presentation
- CAD experience for enclosure design
- Knowledge of power optimization techniques
- Experience with wireless protocols (BLE, WiFi)

You'll be:
- Writing low-level firmware for resource-constrained devices
- Integrating various sensors and peripherals
- Debugging hardware/firmware integration issues
- Optimizing for performance and power consumption
- Building tools for data visualization and analysis
